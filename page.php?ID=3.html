Sunday School


"Sunday School is an important ministry that we all enjoy, even as adults.  This is a crucial time when children learn about God and how He has a special plan for their lives.  In today's world children need somewhere they can come to and not feel the pressure of sin, while knowing there is someone who cares for them in a safe environment.  Children are our future and we must secure the Truth in them.


Our Sunday School program is held every Sunday morning from 9:30 - 10:30.  We sing, play games, watch skits and puppet shows, and most importantly we learn about God through Bible stories and object lessons.  Come and join us in one of our classes: Junior Youth (ages 3-7), Pre-Teens (ages 8-11), Youth Class (ages 12 and up), or the Adult Class.  Be sure not to miss all the fun and exciting things that are to come!"


-Jade Thomas
