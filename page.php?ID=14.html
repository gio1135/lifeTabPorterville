Bro. Josh Webb

Youth

 

"It is our honor to lead the Youth Ministry at Life Tabernacle Apostolic Church of Porterville.

The purpose of the youth ministry is to help provide a safe place for our young people in these last days.  Our number one goal is to help young people get closer to God and further away from an ever changing world.

 

There are many things we participate in throughout the year, including youth prayer meetings, youth services, youth fun and game nights, youth trips, youth conferences, and much more.  We endeavor to keep a spirit of revival alive in our youth group.  God must, and will be, at the forefront of everything we do.  Come join us most Friday nights at 7:00 PM for a fun, spirit-filled night of good and Godly fellowship."

 

-Bro. & Sis. Webb
